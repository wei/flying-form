Why the stack is shaped this way

🔒 Safe by construction

The generator can't emit a broken form

Kimi output is constrained to a closed nine-type enum (text email tel number date select radio checkbox textarea), fence-stripped, JSON.parsed in a try/catch, and Zod-validated before render. Bad output is rejected and retried, never shown.

🗾 Sovereign

Personal data stays on Japan inference

Vision and text both run on Kimi K2.7 on ai& in asia-northeast1. The browser never calls a foreign model API; the proxy forwards server-side. PII on the form is processed in Japan, not shipped abroad.
