01

THE PROBLEM

Japan's buildings
still run on paper.

Re-keyed by hand
Check-in sheets and inspection forms get photographed, re-typed, and filed — every single day. Digitizing one form costs a developer days.
Slow, and wrong
Data sleeps inside the paper. Transcription errors are routine, and the numbers are always late.
Quietly excluding
A Japanese-only sheet is unreadable to foreign visitors and invisible to screen readers. Paper chooses its users.

05

GUARANTEES

Safe by construction. Sovereign by location.

A broken form cannot be generated.
The generator can only emit a closed vocabulary of nine field types. Every output is validated before it renders; invalid output is discarded and retried. A bad photo cannot produce a broken form.
Personal data never leaves Japan.
Every inference runs on Kimi K2.7 via ai&, on Japan-based infrastructure. The browser never calls a foreign model API. PII is processed on domestic inference.
06

INCLUSION

It reaches the people paper excluded.

Foreign visitors
A Japanese-only sheet becomes an English form in one tap — entered values intact.

Anyone at the desk
No app to install. Scan a QR and your own phone becomes the front desk.
